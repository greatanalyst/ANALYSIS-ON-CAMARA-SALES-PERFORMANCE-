# 📸 Camera Sales Analytics

## 🔎 Overview
This project involves the development of an interactive Product Analytics Dashboard focused on the sales performance of 
high-end audio hardware. The dashboard provides a multi-dimensional view of business health, tracking how revenue and 
profit fluctuate across different global markets, timeframes, and customer segments.

## 🎯 Objectives
- Revenue by Country: Top-performing regions with corresponding revenue.  
- Revenue by Date and Year: Comparative trends.  
- Profit and Unit Sales Year-over-Year (YoY) Change: High-level summary of YoY growth.
- Revenue Breakdown by Discount Band: Distribution of revenue across different discount categories. 
- Detailed Table View: Revenue and profit details by country and year.

## 📊 Key Metrics (KPIs)
- Units Sold YoY: 120% increase, indicating a massive surge in market demand.
- Profit YoY: 125% increase, showing that profitability is outpacing unit growth (suggesting better cost management or higher-margin sales).
- Total Revenue by Country: Tracking performance across five major regions, with Canada leading at $98.5k.
- Discount Distribution: Monitoring the percentage of sales falling into "High," "Medium," "Low," and "None" discount bands


## 💡 Insights
- Sector Growth: The Education segment saw the most explosive growth. While profit was modest in 2022 ($150), revenue jumped 
  from $7.7k to $45.3k in 2023, representing a key area for future marketing investment.

- Seasonality: Revenue in 2023 (Blue) shows more stability and growth in the second half of the year compared to the 
  volatile fluctuations seen in 2022 (Grey).

- Discount Sensitivity: 41% of sales are driven by "High" discounts. While this moves volume, only 4.7% of customers are
  willing to pay full price ("None"), suggesting the product is highly price-sensitive or promotionally driven.

- Market Dominance: North American and European markets (Canada, France, Germany) are the primary revenue pillars,
  contributing significantly more than the US market in this specific dataset.
